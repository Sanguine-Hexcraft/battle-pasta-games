Battle Pasta Games is a one-person indie studio making dark, weird, handcrafted games.

## Why "Battle Pasta"

A good question. The answer involves a fever dream, a bowl of cacio e pepe, and a long argument with a friend about whether pasta could canonically defeat a demon. It could. It did.

## What to expect from this devlog

Raw, honest updates. No hype, no PR gloss. Just what's being built, what broke, and what's coming next.
